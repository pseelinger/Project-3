Fruits and Vegetables Are Going Out of Style

A Balanced Diet

Many Americans think of eating healthy as hitting the right amount of calories each day and not going over the standard 2,000 calorie diet. However, a healthy diet is composed of much more that that. A healthy diet requires that you get enough essential nutrients, many of which are found in fruits and vegetables. However, many Americans tend to avoid fruits and vegetables, which can lead to an unbalanced diet and health issues later on.
